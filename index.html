About Me.

Hey! My name is Ankita and I am a Bengali-American student born and raised in Queens, New York. I am currently a sophmore in high school. Some of my interests involve reading, drawing, doing calligraphy and coding! I really enjoy reading new books as it immerses me into whole different worlds. I enjoy practicing art, color pencil realism -- I love blending and piecing colors together in creating the end result. I started coding back during quarantine, and I fell in love ever since. The thought of how different elements come together to create an interactive or creative product fascinates and excites me.

I also enjoy hanging out with my friends and love trying new foods and desserts with them. Throughout the week I often volunteer at my local library; it's honestly really fun to just simply explore the many things I haven't encountered before. That's a little bit about me! My personal website encompasses some of my hobbies and interests. I plan on adding new things every once in a while so it stays close with me! Stick around if you'd like :))
